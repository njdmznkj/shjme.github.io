Huijie Sun's
Biography

Inner Mongolian
Operatic Mezzo-soprano

Currently studying at Peabody institute of John Hopkins University doing her Graduate Performance Diploma under the guidance of Prof. Stanley Cornett. Sun grew up in Hohhot, the capital city of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, China. In 2022, she won the Second prize of the professional category in the London Classical Music Competition and the Third prize of the professional level in the London International music competition.
